EgBrake Brake Calculator

This data editor is compatible to the EgBrake 2.17.x databases.
Do not try to edit any earlier or later database versions with this editor as doing so will most certainly destroy your data and make it unusable.
The different screen masks will be loaded on first selection. Until fully loaded you will see a short message in the top left corner of the centre screen. Once loaded the mask will be displayed and you can view and - if your user account is authorized accordingly - edit data.
